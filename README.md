# AI Cost Reality

**Is your AI subscription worth it?**

A bilingual (EN/JA) calculator that shows what your AI subscriptions really cost — compared to hiring humans. Based on 50 days of real data from a non-engineer running Claude Code autonomously.

**[Calculate Your AI Cost](https://yurukusa.github.io/ai-cost-reality/)**

## What It Does

1. Select your AI subscriptions (ChatGPT, Claude, Copilot, Cursor, Midjourney, etc.)
2. Get your total monthly/annual cost
3. See per-hour, per-session, and per-commit breakdowns
4. Compare to hiring a junior developer or freelancer
5. See what $200/month actually produced in 50 days
